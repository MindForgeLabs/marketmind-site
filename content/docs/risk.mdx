# Risk

Current MarketMind risk language is about research governance and validation gates. Execution risk controls are planned, not shipped.

## Current Gates

- Leakage checks and time-series validation discipline.
- Statistical thresholds before promotion.
- Deterministic artifact review.
- Canonical provenance for promoted bundles.
- Governance review before claims are treated as durable.

## Planned Controls

Position limits, drawdown throttles, kill switches, live PnL monitoring, and broker-side execution controls remain future runtime work.

## Claim Discipline

Do not describe live risk controls as current until a live execution stack exists and has measured operational evidence.
