# Strategies

Strategy work in MarketMind should be framed as governed research until the evidence supports promotion.

## Current Strategy Boundary

A strategy candidate must be evaluated with:

- Point-in-time-safe input data.
- Leakage-aware splits and purge/embargo discipline.
- Deterministic artifacts.
- Canonical run bundle provenance.
- Statistical and governance gates.

## What Must Be Proven

MarketMind should not claim that an adaptive strategy or allocator is superior until it beats a simpler baseline under the same validation discipline.

## Planned Execution Work

Paper trading, live market data, broker execution, C++ runtime deployment, and live risk controls are Phase III style work. They are not available as current product capability.
